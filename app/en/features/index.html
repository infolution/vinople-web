Vinople is an application for winery management.

It enables you to record every action in your winery and replaces all those unbounded notes you made before.

Features

Complete winery picture

You can be aware of your winery situation with one view.

Fast answers

What sugar level was before fermentation? How much did acid levels vary? These are only a fraction of questions for which we can get answers in any moment. Use this to make better decisions in your production.

Traceability

It is very important to know the origin of every bottle in your winery. Trace origin through every phase of production, even to the vineyard from which the bottle came.

Cost of production

During production of wine all kinds of expenses rise up, some of which you don't even pay attention. Wouldn't it be great to have those expenses included in your production cost. At every moment.

Work anywhere

Working in the winery is not always tied to the office. You must be by the tanks, in the vineyard, with your customers.

What Vinople tracks

  • Input of grapes, must, wine
  • Fermentation
  • Maceration
  • Aging
  • Wine transfers
  • Analysis
  • Supplements
  • Output of wine
  • Warehouse
  • Product warehouse
  • Production cost
